==================================================
PROJECT VISION
==================================================

TWINS ERP is an institutional-grade Fuel Trading, Logistics, Inventory, Settlement, Operations, and Profitability platform built in Laravel.

It is designed specifically for fuel trading businesses operating products such as:

- AGO (Diesel)
- PMS (Petrol)
- Jet Fuel
- Other petroleum products

The long-term goal is to operate an entire fuel company from purchase to final profitability analysis.

This is NOT:

- a simple inventory system
- a basic accounting package
- a generic ERP

The system must accurately model how fuel businesses actually operate.

==================================================

Business purchases fuel.

Fuel is transported by international transporters.

Fuel may then be transported by local transporters.

==================================================
PURCHASE MODES
==================================================

==================================================
1. CROSS DOCK
==================================================

Business meaning:

Fuel already exists inside a loaded truck.

Ownership transfers immediately.

Workflow:

Draft
→ Confirm

On confirm:

- batch created
- inventory receipt posted
- stock enters CROSS DOCK depot
- stock immediately available for sale

No receive step.

==================================================
2. LOCAL DEPOT
==================================================

Business meaning:

Fuel already exists inside a depot.

Only ownership changes.

Workflow:

Draft
→ Confirm
→ Receive

Confirm:

- create batch
- no inventory movement

Receive:

- inventory receipt posted
- stock enters depot

==================================================
3. IMPORT
==================================================

Import is a complete operational workflow.

Purpose:

Track fuel from purchase until physical offload.

Workflow concept:

Purchase
→ Nominations
→ Loading
→ Transit
→ Border
→ Clearance
→ Offload
→ Inventory Receipt

Inventory enters stock ONLY after offload.

==================================================
INTERNATIONAL TRANSPORTERS
==================================================

Transporters are paid:

Rate per m³ loaded.

System must track:

- nominations
- loaded quantities
- delivered quantities
- shortages
- allowances
- advances
- recoveries
- payments

Transporter payable formula:

Gross Freight
=
Loaded Qty × Freight Rate

Net Payable
=
Gross Freight
− Short Charges
− Recoveries
− Advances
− Payments

==================================================
SHORT CHARGE RULES
==================================================

Actual Short
-
Allowable Short
=
Chargeable Short

Chargeable Short
×
Short Rate
=
Short Charge

Must support:

- Global short rate
- Trip override

Never hardcode.

==================================================
INVENTORY PERIODS
==================================================

Inventory periods are OPTIONAL.

System supports:

==================================================
SIMPLE MODE
==================================================

No periods.

Continuous posting.

Costing controlled by:

companies.costing_method

==================================================
CONTROLLED MODE
==================================================

Inventory periods enabled.

Postings belong to periods.

Periods can be closed.

Historical periods become immutable.

Costing changes happen through period transitions.

==================================================
COSTING CHANGE RULE
==================================================

If company has NO inventory movements:

Costing method may be changed directly.

If company HAS inventory movements:

Costing method becomes locked.

Changing costing requires:

Inventory Period Wizard.
